The Opportunity

Flag Football is Exploding

Flag football is the fastest-growing sport in America. With its 2028 Olympics debut, participation is surging — yet there's no dominant digital platform serving this massive community. OnlyFlags fills that gap.

📈
Explosive Growth

Flag football participation grew 62% since 2019. The NFL has invested $100M+ into flag programs. NFL FLAG has 700,000+ registered youth players alone. The market is massive and underserved digitally.

🏅
Olympics Catalyst

The 2028 LA Olympics will feature flag football for the first time. Global viewership estimated at 3+ billion. This will create unprecedented mainstream attention and a wave of new players worldwide.

💎

The Product

A Complete Sports Ecosystem

OnlyFlags is not just an app — it's the entire digital layer for flag football. Community, gameplay, economy, and blockchain — unified in one platform.

🏈
Team Management

How it works: Players request or join local teams, invite friends, and get matched by city/state. Referees log real-time game stats. Admins approve team formations and manage leagues.

💎
12 NFT Types × 5 Rarities

How it works: NFTs are earned automatically (attendance, achievements, milestones) or purchased. Player Cards level up with XP from real game performance. Rarity determines staking yield and marketplace value. Off-chain first, deployable on Polygon.

🪙
FLAG Token Economy

How it works: Earn FLAG tokens by playing, attending events, sharing content, completing challenges, and referring friends. Spend on NFT marketplace, auction bids, fantasy entry fees, tournament fees, merch, and premium subscriptions. Staking NFTs generates daily passive token yield.

🏆
Tournaments & Fantasy

How it works: Organized tournaments (single/double elimination, round robin) with token entry fees and prize pools. Daily fantasy contests with real flag football stats. Prediction markets on game outcomes. Platform takes 10% rake on all pools.

🛍️
Marketplace & Commerce

How it works: Built-in NFT marketplace (5% fee), English/Dutch/fixed-price auctions, print-on-demand merch store (35% margin), and creator marketplace where users sell highlight compilations and playbooks (20% platform cut + 5% perpetual royalties).

📱
Social & Viral Growth

How it works: Share NFTs, highlights, and achievements across Instagram, TikTok, X, Facebook, YouTube, and Snapchat — earning tokens for each share. Share streaks multiply rewards (3-day: 1.5x, 7-day: 2x, 30-day: 3x). 3-tier affiliate program with multi-level commissions drives organic growth.
